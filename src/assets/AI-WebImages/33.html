What We Offer

AI Planning

AI Planning is an intelligent logistics planning system designed to manage the complete journey from data ingestion to real-time dispatch and execution. It connects multiple data sources such as orders, driver apps, telematics systems, sensors, and existing enterprise systems into a unified platform. Using a custom AI processing layer, the system generates demand from historical data, creates optimized orders and trips, assigns the right drivers and trucks, and continuously monitors execution using live traffic and operational data. The platform also supports admin approvals, exception handling, ETA management, and real-time re-planning, ensuring operations stay efficient and on track.

How It Works

Stage 1: Dynamic Orders

Ingests regular and emergency orders and enriches them with optimization-ready data like locations, time windows, and constraints.

Stage 2: Optimized Trips

Builds efficient multi-drop trips using AI routing, live traffic, risk scoring, and operational constraints.

Stage 3: Intelligent Resource Assignment

Assigns the right truck and driver based on compatibility, certifications, capacity, and real-time status.

Stage 4: Real-Time Sequential Dispatch

Monitors execution, tracks progress, handles exceptions, and dynamically re-optimizes plans in real time.

The Self-Correcting Learning Loop: Continuous Improvement Engine

Execution data continuously feeds back into the AI models to refine forecasting, routing, and planning for future operations.
